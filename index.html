    What do these metrics mean?

    CPM: cost to show your ad 1,000 times. Lower = you’re reaching people efficiently.

    CTR: % of people who click. Low CTR means the ad isn’t catching attention.

    CPC: what each click costs. High CPC = try new audiences or creative.

    Frequency: how often the same person sees the ad. Too high = ad fatigue.
